"Multo" (Ghost) by Cup of Joe is a synth-pop/pop-rock ballad about being haunted by the lingering memories, pain, and longing of a past relationship or past version of oneself. It explores the difficulty of moving on when memories still resurface, treating these memories as emotional "ghosts" that haunt the present.
